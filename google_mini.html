Factory Reset your google_home mini

Connect to your home network

Required list of the materials....

  • Google Home Mini

  • Android or iphone device

  • Install Google Home App on the your device

    • Once you have all the above devices and ready to reset the device.

  • First Step -> Make sure the power is on and light on the device is also on.

  • Second step -> Flip your google_home device and check on it's back

You will see the button on back of your google_home device

    Now Press the button for 5-7 seconds, until the light of your google_home device change to orange. Now just wait till the device reboot itself.

  • Third Step -> Once you listen the Google Assistance voice, we just need to follow the instruction.

  • Fourth step -> Download and install the google_home app on your mobile phone.

Once you install the google_home app

    Now follow the instraction of the app... Very first Connect to your gmail account. Then make sure your mobile bluetooth is on.

  • Fifth Step -> Allow the location share, then it will show the the device is connected to your device. As soon as it connected to the device it will make a sound then request you to aggree the policy

  • sixth step -> Give name to the device (optional) then it will request you to connect to the home network (wifi) you have to enter the correct password selecting the correct network..

Once google_mini is connected to the home network

    Just give couple of voice command to train the device. Go to the "account setting" (on top right corner). Account setting and device settings are completly different. If you want to change the assitance voice (Male/Female). Follow the steps.....

  • First Step -> Open the gmail account setting (top right corner), then go to "Assistance Setting" --> "Assistance Voice"

  • Second step -> Now, Select the Different Option and listen to the voice you prefer then just go back to main page. --> It will be automatically save the setting and voice will be changed as per the selection.

    Now enjoy the music and magic of the smart device (google_home_mini).
